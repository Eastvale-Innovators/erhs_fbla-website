Community Service

At ERHS FBLA, we are dedicated to helping our communities.

We work with the City, Jurupa Community Services District (JCSD), and many other businesses throughout Eastvale to implement our Annual Chapter Projects and Fundraisers.

Annual Fundraiser

This is a month-long event through which we raise money for various goals: from ensuring reproductive health to filling up food banks.

We spread the word throughout our communities and work with organizations such as March of Dimes and Feeding America.
